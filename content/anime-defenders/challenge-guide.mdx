## Best Teams for Challenges

### Challenge Tower
**Gojo + Sukuna + Orihime** — This team provides the best balance of damage and survivability for tower progression.

### Raid Bosses
**Madara + Zaraki + Elisabeth** — Maximum DPS output with support healing to survive boss attacks.

### Time Attack
**Levi + Escanor + Sakura** — Speed-focused composition for clearing stages within time limits.

## General Challenge Tips

1. **Evolve before attempting** — Evolved defenders are significantly stronger
2. **Optimize traits** — Use damage-boosting traits for DPS defenders
3. **Bring a healer** — Support defenders with healing are essential for hard content
4. **Learn boss patterns** — Raid bosses have attack patterns you can dodge
5. **Use Gems wisely** — Save for rate-up banners that feature challenge-viable defenders

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What power level do I need for Challenge Tower?**
Aim for at least 25,000 power before attempting Challenge Tower floors above 50.

**Can I beat raids solo?**
Difficult but possible with a fully evolved S-tier team. Co-op is recommended for most players.
