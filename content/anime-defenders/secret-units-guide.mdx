## Tips for Getting Secret Defenders

1. **Save Gems** — Don't spend on normal banners, wait for secret rate-up events
2. **Complete Challenges** — Some secrets are locked behind specific achievements
3. **Participate in Events** — Limited-time events are the only way to get certain secrets
4. **Join a Guild** — Guild rewards sometimes include secret defender fragments

## Are Secret Defenders Worth It?

Absolutely. Secret defenders in Anime Defenders are significantly stronger than even evolved S-tier defenders. If you're serious about endgame content, obtaining at least one secret defender should be a priority.
