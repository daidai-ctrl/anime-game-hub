## Common Beginner Mistakes

- **Spreading resources too thin** — Focus on 2-3 defenders instead of upgrading everything
- **Ignoring evolution materials** — Start farming evolution items early
- **Skipping daily missions** — These are your most consistent resource income
- **Not using codes** — Free Gems and items from codes give a huge early boost

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What should I spend Gems on first?**
Save your Gems for the premium banner or evolution materials. Avoid spending on basic items you can farm.
