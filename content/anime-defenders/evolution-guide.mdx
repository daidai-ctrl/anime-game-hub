## Anime Defenders Evolution Guide (2026) — All Evolutions & Materials

Evolution is the most powerful upgrade system in Anime Defenders. Evolved defenders gain massive stat boosts and new abilities, making them significantly stronger than their base forms.

## All Evolutions in Anime Defenders

| Base | Evolved | Materials | Bonus |
|------|---------|-----------|-------|
| Gojo | **Gojo Unlimited** | 10 Cursed Energy + 5 Domain Shards | 2x Damage, New Ultimate |
| Sukuna | **Sukuna Domain** | 15 Malevolent Energy + 8 Finger Fragments | 3x AoE, Domain Skill |
| Madara | **Madara Susanoo** | 12 Sharingan Essences + 6 Chakra Crystals | 2.5x Damage, Susanoo Skill |
| Zaraki | **Zaraki Bankai** | 8 Soul Reapers + 4 Spirit Crystals | 2x Crit, Bankai Skill |
| Tanjiro | **Tanjiro Sun Breathing** | 6 Demon Blood + 3 Sun Cores | 2x Crit, Sun Breathing |

## Evolution Priority

1. **Gojo → Gojo Unlimited** — Highest damage increase, essential for endgame
2. **Sukuna → Sukuna Domain** — Massive AoE upgrade, great for farming
3. **Madara → Madara Susanoo** — Strong PvP evolution with new skills

## How to Farm Evolution Materials

- **Daily Dungeons** — Consistent source of evolution materials
- **Raid Bosses** — Higher drop rates for rare materials
- **Events** — Limited-time events often provide evolution items
- **Shop** — Some materials can be purchased with Gold

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which defender should I evolve first?**
Evolve Gojo first — the Gojo Unlimited form is a massive upgrade that carries in all content.

**Can I undo an evolution?**
No, evolutions are permanent. Make sure you want to evolve before committing the materials.
