## The Evolution System in Anime Last Stand

Evolution is one of the most important systems in Anime Last Stand. Evolved units receive massive stat boosts, new skills, and visual upgrades that make them significantly stronger than their base forms.

Most top-tier units in Anime Last Stand require evolution to reach their full potential. This guide covers every evolution available in the game, including materials needed and the best evolution priority order.

## Evolution Priority Guide

When you have limited materials, evolve in this order:

1. **Gojo → Gojo Unleashed** — The strongest evolution in the game. Do this first.
2. **Sukuna → Sukuna Domain Expansion** — Incredible AoE damage and domain skill.
3. **Madara → Madara Susanoo** — Susanoo skill is game-changing.
4. **Luffy → Luffy Gear 5** — Gear 5 transformation is excellent.
5. **Tanjiro → Tanjiro Sun Breathing** — Great for budget teams.

## How to Get Evolution Materials

1. **Raid Bosses** — The primary source of evolution materials
2. **Challenge Tower** — Higher floors drop rare materials
3. **Daily Dungeons** — Consistent material farming
4. **Events** — Limited events often provide bonus materials
5. **Trading** — Some materials can be traded with guild members

## Evolution Tips

- Always check material requirements before spending resources
- Focus on evolving your main DPS first for the biggest power spike
- Some evolutions require clearing specific story chapters first
- Evolution materials are shared across units — plan carefully
- Use evolution crystals from codes for a free evolution

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best evolution in Anime Last Stand?**
Gojo → Gojo Unleashed is the strongest evolution in Anime Last Stand for 2026.

**How long does it take to evolve a unit?**
On average, 1-2 weeks of focused material farming. Events and codes can speed this up significantly.

No, evolutions are permanent. Make sure you want to evolve before committing materials.
