The trait system in Arise Crossover allows you to customize your characters with passive bonuses that enhance their performance. Choosing the right trait can dramatically improve effectiveness in combat, farming, and PvP.

## How to Get Good Traits

1. **Trait Reroll** — Use Crystals or special items to reroll traits
2. **Farming** — Some game modes drop trait enhancement items
3. **Events** — Limited-time events often reward premium trait items
4. **Evolution Bonuses** — Some evolutions unlock exclusive traits

## Trait Priority Guide

- **DPS Characters** — Prioritize damage-boosting traits like Overlord or Demon King
- **Support Characters** — Look for healing or buff-enhancing traits
- **Hybrid Characters** — Balanced traits that improve both offense and defense
