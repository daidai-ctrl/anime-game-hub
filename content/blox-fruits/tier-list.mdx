## Blox Fruits Tier List - May 2026

Our comprehensive tier list ranks every fruit in Blox Fruits based on PvP and PvE performance. This list is updated for Update 26, including the new Eagle and Creation fruits, and reworked Gravity and Flame.

## S-Tier (Best Fruits)

These are the absolute best fruits in the game right now:

- **Dragon** - Incredible damage output and mobility
- **Leopard** - High burst damage and combo potential
- **Venom** - Excellent AoE damage and DoT effects
- **Dough** - Amazing for both PvP and PvE
- **Oni** - Buffed in recent update, devastating PvP potential

## A-Tier (Great Fruits)

Strong choices that perform well in most situations:

- **Buddha** - Tanky with great grind potential
- **Magma** - Excellent for PvE grinding
- **Eagle** (New - formerly Falcon) - Reworked with enhanced aerial combat
- **Creation** (New - formerly Barrier) - Versatile defensive and offensive abilities
- **Quake** - Good crowd control abilities
- **Rumble** - Versatile with decent damage

## B-Tier (Good Fruits)

Solid picks that can work well with the right build:

- **Dark** - Reworked with improved stuns and combo potential
- **Gravity** - Reworked in Update 26, improved utility
- **Ice** - Nice crowd control
- **Light** - Fast travel and decent damage
- **Flame** - Reworked in Update 26, improved model and abilities

## C-Tier (Average Fruits)

These fruits are okay but outclassed by higher tiers:

- **Rubber** - Niche uses
- **Spring** - Limited utility
- **Bomb** - Outdated kit
- **Chop** - Good for specific PvP builds but limited elsewhere

## D-Tier (Weak Fruits)

Not recommended for serious play:

- **Kilo** - Very limited abilities
- **Spin** - Minimal damage output

## How We Rank Fruits

Our rankings consider:
1. **PvP Performance** - How effective in player battles
2. **PvE/Grinding** - Speed and efficiency in farming
3. **Versatility** - Usefulness across different scenarios
4. **Skill Ceiling** - Potential in skilled hands

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best fruit in Blox Fruits?
Currently, Dragon and Leopard are considered the best fruits overall due to their high damage output and versatility. Oni has also risen in the rankings after recent buffs.

### How often is the tier list updated?
We update our tier list after every major game update or balance change, typically every 2-4 weeks.

### Is the new Eagle fruit worth using?
Yes! The reworked Eagle fruit (formerly Falcon) has significantly improved aerial combat abilities and is now a strong A-tier option, especially for players who prefer hit-and-run tactics.
