## Blox Fruits Swords Tier List (2025)

Swords are a core combat option in Blox Fruits. This tier list ranks every sword based on PvP viability, PvE effectiveness, and overall utility.

## S-Tier Swords (Best in Game)

| Sword | Type | Why S-Tier |
|-------|------|------------|
| Yoru (Dark Blade) | Legendary | Highest damage, best combo potential |
| Spikey Trident | Legendary | Excellent PvP moveset, stun combos |
| Hallow Scythe | Mythical | Massive AoE, great for grinding |

## A-Tier Swords (Excellent)

| Sword | Type | Why A-Tier |
|-------|------|------------|
| Tushita | Legendary | Fast moves, good combo starter |
| Yama | Legendary | High damage, decent range |
| Shisui | Rare | Solid all-rounder |
| Wando | Rare | Good for beginners |

## Best Swords for Different Playstyles

### Best for PvP
Yoru (Dark Blade) + Spikey Trident - The combination provides the best combo potential and burst damage.

### Best for PvE/Grinding
Hallow Scythe - Large AoE attacks clear mobs efficiently.

### Best for Beginners
Shisui - Easy to obtain and effective at all levels.

## How to Get the Best Swords

- **Yoru**: Defeat Mihawk Boss or trade (very rare)
- **Spikey Trident**: Fishman Island drop
- **Hallow Scythe**: Halloween Event or trade
