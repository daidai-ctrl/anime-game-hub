Blox Fruits Update 30 is shaping up to be one of the most significant content drops in the game's history. With developer hints, in-game leaks, and community observations all pointing toward major changes, here is everything we know so far.

## What Is Coming in Update 30

### New Fruits: Magnet, Oni, Celestial, Shadow, and More

Developer Shaffy Dev revealed that the five fruits currently in development include **Magnet**, **Oni**, **Celestial**, **Shadow**, and a fifth "Wukong themed" fruit. If accurate, this is the most comprehensive development roadmap the community has ever received from an insider source.

**Magnet Fruit** is currently the strongest candidate for Update 30. Developer hints mentioning "metal gears" match magnet-themed imagery, and Shaffy Dev reportedly reacted positively to community speculation about the Magnet Fruit. Expected abilities include pull effects, metal constructs, area control, and flashy attack visuals.

**Oni and Celestial** are generating the most community excitement. Both have been teased for some time, with in-game helmets tied to both fruits appearing as event rewards in 2025. Multiple game admins have been spotted using the Oni fruit in their profiles:

- **Oni** — demon-themed beast transformation with high-damage, aggressive combat style. Expected Mythical rarity.
- **Celestial** — divine, light-based transformation. One confirmed move from game files is called "Karmatic Shatter," a large AOE ability fitting the holy aesthetic of Divine Art.

Both fruits are expected to carry Mythical rarity, meaning near-zero spawn rates through the fruit dealer, and their trading value on release day will likely be extreme.

### Venom Rework

The Venom Rework is **officially confirmed** by the Blox Fruits development team for Update 30. The rework includes:

- **New passive: Poison Stack System** — each Venom attack applies a "poison stack" to targets. Stacks accumulate with consecutive hits, and DOT damage scales with stack count
- **Vertical mobility addition** — Venom's biggest weakness has been no meaningful aerial option. A vertical movement ability directly addresses this gap
- **Visual overhaul** — new attack animations and effects for the fruit's appearance
- **Individual move changes** — Z Move rumored to become wide-area spray, X Move possible speedup (~15 frames faster startup) plus slow effect

The rework shifts Venom from a "one heavy hit" fruit to a combo-sustain fruit where staying on your target matters.

### Dark Fruit Rework

Also confirmed for Update 30. Dark has been in the game for years without meaningful updates. Community speculation centers on a possible rename to **Void fruit**, though that remains unconfirmed. Expect visual and animation upgrades — not necessarily a major power spike.

### Fourth Sea: Teased But Not Here Yet

Admin Uzoth shared an image showing the full map layout of the first three seas, with observers noticing a massive unexplained landmass on one side. The terrain appears to feature red mountain-like environments.

However, the current community consensus points toward a **Fourth Sea launch in late 2026** (possibly December), not Update 30. Fourth Sea is being built, but Update 30 is more likely to focus on fruit reworks and new fruits first.
