## Blox Fruits Beginner Guide

Starting out in Blox Fruits can be overwhelming. This guide covers everything you need to know to get started on the right foot.

## Getting Started

When you first join Blox Fruits, you'll spawn on the starter island. Here's what to do first:

1. **Talk to the Quest NPC** - Accept your first quest to start earning XP
2. **Learn the Combat Style** - Choose between Melee, Sword, or Gun fighting styles
3. **Save your Beli** - Don't spend on random items early on

## Leveling Up Fast

### Early Game (Level 1-100)
- Focus on completing quests in order
- Use codes for free XP boosts
- Join a crew for bonus XP

### Mid Game (Level 100-500)
- Farm bosses for rare drops
- Start collecting fruits
- Participate in raids

### Late Game (Level 500+)
- Focus on PvP builds
- Collect legendary fruits
- Help newer players for crew XP

### End Game (Level 2600-2650)
- Master the new Fruit Progression System from Update 26
- Farm Sea Events for exclusive rewards
- Trade high-value fruits with other players
- Take on the hardest raid bosses

## Best Fruits for Beginners

If you're just starting out, these fruits are easy to use and effective:

1. **Flame** - Reworked in Update 26, improved abilities and visual model
2. **Ice** - Great crowd control for grinding
3. **Light** - Fast movement helps with quests

## Tips and Tricks

- Always check for new codes before playing
- Use stat refund codes to reset your build
