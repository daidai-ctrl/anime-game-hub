## Understanding the Trait System in Blox Fruits

The trait system in Blox Fruits allows you to customize your fruits with passive bonuses that enhance their performance. Choosing the right trait can dramatically improve a fruit's effectiveness in combat, farming, and PvP.

Each fruit can equip one trait, and some traits are significantly better than others depending on the fruit's role and your team composition.

## Best Traits by Unit Role

### Best Traits for DPS Units
- **Superhuman** — The go-to trait for damage dealers. Increases overall damage output.
- **Electric Claw** — Great for burst-focused DPS that rely on skill combos.

### Best Traits for Support Units
- **Death Step** — Amplifies buff effects, making your support even more valuable.
- **Sharkman Karate** — Increases survivability so supports stay alive longer.

### Best Traits for PvP
- **Dragon Talon** — Speed advantage is crucial in PvP for outpacing opponents.

## How to Unlock and Upgrade Traits

1. **Unlocking Traits** — Traits are unlocked through fruits progression and specific achievements
2. **Upgrading Traits** — Use duplicate fruits or special upgrade materials
3. **Rerolling Traits** — Some traits can be rerolled using premium currency
4. **Trait Synergies** — Some traits have bonus effects when paired with specific fruits

## Tips for Choosing the Right Trait

1. **Match the trait to the fruit's role** — Don't put a DPS trait on a support
2. **Consider team composition** — Some traits benefit from team synergy
3. **Test before committing** — Try different traits in easy content first
4. **S-tier traits are always worth the investment** — They provide the biggest stat boost
5. **Save reroll resources for your main team** — Don't waste them on bench fruits
6. **Check meta builds** — Top players share optimal trait setups

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best trait in Blox Fruits?**
Superhuman is generally considered the best overall trait in Blox Fruits for 2026, as it provides the most universal damage bonus.

**Can I change traits later?**
Yes, traits can be changed, but it may cost premium currency or rare materials. Plan your trait choices carefully.

**Do traits stack with buffs?**
Yes, trait bonuses are multiplicative with buff effects, which is why choosing the right trait is so important for maximizing team performance.
