## Blox Fruits Dragon Rework Guide (May 2026)

The Dragon fruit rework in Update 24 completely transformed one of Blox Fruits' most iconic fruits. Gone is the old Dragon — in its place is a dual-form powerhouse with Eastern and Western variants, a new Fury Meter system, and some of the highest damage resistance in the entire game.

**TL;DR:** Dragon is **S-Tier** in the May 2026 meta. It costs 15,000,000 Beli or 5,000 Robux. Pick Eastern for survivability (smaller hitbox), Western for speed. The Fury Meter system is the key mechanic — learn to manage it and you'll be nearly unkillable in full dragon form with 60% damage resistance.

## Fury Meter System Explained

The Fury Meter is the core mechanic that separates good Dragon players from great ones. Understanding how it fills and drains is essential.

### Orange Fury Meter (Hybrid Form)

The orange meter fills over time as you play. When it's full, you can activate **Imperial Evolution** to transform into your hybrid dragon form. In this state:

- Gain **20% PvP / 34% NPC damage resistance**
- Enhanced moveset with burning effect on M1 attacks
- Meter drains when you use moves

### Red Fury Meter (Full Dragon Form)

The red meter fills by **dealing damage** to enemies. When full, you transform into a complete dragon — this is where Dragon fruit truly shines:

- Gain **60% damage resistance** to all attacks
- Full flight capability
- Can move during crowd control attacks
- Meter drains when taking damage (2x faster on headshots)
- Killing an opponent within 50 levels **restores meter**

> ⚠️ **Common Mistake:** Don't waste your Fury Meter by spamming moves in hybrid form. Save your red meter for full dragon transformation — that 60% damage resistance is what makes Dragon borderline unkillable. Use hybrid form to build the red meter, then transform when you're ready to fight.

## Eastern vs Western Dragon

One of the biggest additions in the rework is the choice between two dragon variants. Each has distinct advantages.

### 🐉 Eastern Dragon
- Smaller hitbox — harder to hit
- Better for PvP survivability
- Carry up to 4 allies
- Serpentine movement style
- **Best for:** PvP, 1v1 fights

### 🔥 Western Dragon
- Faster travel speed
- Better for map mobility
- Carry up to 5 allies
- Bulkier, more imposing form
- **Best for:** PvE grinding, raids

**Our recommendation:** If you PvP often, go Eastern. The smaller hitbox makes a real difference when skilled players are trying to land combos on you. If you're mostly grinding and doing raids, Western's speed saves you real time traveling between islands.

## Passive Abilities & Skins

### Mount System (500 Mastery)

At 500 mastery, talk to the **Dragon Tamer NPC** and pay 5,000 Beli to unlock the Strong Hair/Saddle ability. This lets allies mount you during transformation — 4 max for Eastern, 5 max for Western. This is incredibly useful for carrying lower-level friends through raids or traveling together.

## FAQ

### How do I get the Dragon fruit in Blox Fruits?
The Dragon fruit can be purchased from the Blox Fruit Dealer for 15,000,000 Beli or 5,000 Robux. It also spawns naturally on the map every hour (despawns after 20 minutes). As a Mythical Beast-type fruit, it appears very rarely in stock rotation — check back every 4 hours when the dealer restocks.

### What is the difference between Eastern and Western Dragon?
Eastern Dragon has a smaller hitbox making it harder for opponents to hit you, and can carry up to 4 allies. Western Dragon travels faster and can carry up to 5 allies. Both share the same moveset but offer different strategic advantages. Eastern is better for PvP; Western is better for PvE and mobility.

### Is Dragon fruit worth it in Blox Fruits 2026?
Absolutely. Dragon is currently S-tier in the May 2026 meta. It excels in PvE with massive AOE clearing and in PvP with 60% damage resistance in full dragon form. Combined with Draco race synergies, it's ranked #2 overall behind Kitsune. At 15M Beli it's expensive, but the power justifies the price.

### How does the Fury Meter work?
Dragon has two Fury Meters. The orange meter fills over time and enables hybrid transformation when full. The red meter fills by dealing damage and enables full dragon transformation. Using moves drains the orange meter; taking damage drains both. Headshots drain meters 2x faster. Killing an opponent within 50 levels restores some meter.

### What fighting style pairs best with Dragon?
Death Step is the best pairing for competitive PvP. The combo "Dragon Fury → Death Step Vermillion Drill → Pole V2 Assault → Dragon Heatwave Cannon → Death Step Barrage" deals ~16,200 damage at max stats. For PvE grinding, Electric Claw offers faster M1 attack speed.
