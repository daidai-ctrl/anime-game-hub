## Dragon Rework Overview

The Dragon Rework in Blox Fruits Update 26 completely overhauled the Dragon fruit, making it one of the most powerful fruits in the game. Dragon V2 features new abilities, improved transformations, and devastating combos.

## Dragon V2 Abilities

### Base Form
- **Dragon Breath** - A concentrated fire breath attack with increased range
- **Dragon Claw** - Enhanced melee attack with knockback
- **Dragon Flight** - Improved mobility with faster flight speed

### Transformation (V2)
- **Azure Dragon Form** - Full dragon transformation with wing attacks
- **Dragon Storm** - AoE fire tornado that deals massive damage
- **Dragon Roar** - Stun + damage combo opener

## Best Combos

### PvP Combo 1: Dragon Storm Setup
1. Dragon Roar (stun)
2. Dragon Storm (AoE damage)
3. Dragon Claw (finisher)

### PvP Combo 2: Hit and Run
1. Dragon Flight (approach)
2. Dragon Breath (range poke)
3. Dragon Claw (close range)
4. Dragon Flight (retreat)

## Is Dragon V2 Worth It?

**Absolutely.** Dragon V2 is currently ranked S-tier in our tier list. The rework made it viable for both PvP and PvE content. If you have the opportunity to obtain Dragon, do not hesitate.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get Dragon Fruit in Blox Fruits?
Dragon Fruit can be obtained through the Blox Fruit Dealer for 3,500,000 Beli or as a random spawn. It can also be obtained through trading with other players.

### Is Dragon V2 better than Dragon V1?
Yes, significantly. Dragon V2 has improved abilities, better combo potential, and higher damage output compared to V1.

### What is the best race for Dragon V2?
Ghoul or Cyborg are the best races for Dragon V2 due to their damage bonuses and survivability.
