## Blox Fruits Leveling Guide

Reaching max level in Blox Fruits takes time, but with the right strategy, you can significantly speed up the process. This guide covers the fastest leveling route from Level 1 to 2650.

## Level 1-100: First Sea Beginner

### Best Farming Spots
- **Level 1-15**: Bandits at Pirate Village
- **Level 15-30**: Gorillas at Jungle
- **Level 30-60**: Snow Troopers at Winter Village
- **Level 60-100**: Skylands Mobs

### Tips
- Use a fruit with AoE attacks for faster farming
- Accept quests from quest givers for bonus XP
- Server hop if your farming spot is crowded

## Level 100-400: First Sea Intermediate

### Best Farming Spots
- **Level 100-200**: Dark Arena / Prison
- **Level 200-300**: Underwater City
- **Level 300-400**: Skylands Upper Area

## Level 400-800: Second Sea

### Best Farming Spots

- **Level 625-750**: Graveyard

### Best Farming Spots

- **Level 1250-1500**: Forgotten Island

- **Level 1775-2000**: Mansion
- **Level 2000-2250**: Hydra Island
- **Level 2250-2650**: Tiki Outpost / Sea Events

## General Leveling Tips

1. **Always have an active quest** - Quests give 2-3x more XP than grinding without
2. **Use 2x XP codes** - Check our [Blox Fruits Codes](/blox-fruits/codes) page for active XP boosts
3. **Farm with friends** - Party farming increases spawn rates
4. **Server hop** - Fresh servers have more mob spawns
5. **Use Buddha Fruit** - Buddha's hitbox makes farming significantly easier
