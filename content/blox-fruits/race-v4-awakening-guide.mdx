Race V4 Awakening represents the ultimate evolution path for any race in Blox Fruits. This transformation grants extraordinary combat bonuses, enhanced mobility, and race-specific abilities that fundamentally change how you approach both player-versus-player combat and PvE grinding sessions.

This guide walks you through every requirement, puzzle step, and trial challenge needed to unlock your race's full potential.

## Prerequisites Before Starting

Before attempting Race V4 Awakening, you must complete several preparation steps:

**Required Checklist:**
- Access to the Third Sea (reach level 1750+)
- Your current race at Version 3 (V3)
- Rip Indra defeated at least once
- One Mirror Fractal obtained from the Dough King

Rip Indra is a level 5,000 raid boss located at Castle on the Sea. To summon him, you'll need three legendary Aura Colors (Snow White, Pure Red, and Winter Sky) plus a God's Chalice. Team composition matters significantly—bring a Buddha user for tanking and high-DPS players to handle damage output. Consider using a private server to avoid exploiters.

## Step-by-Step Unlock Process

### 1. Initiating the Sealed King Quest

Head to Castle on the Sea and locate the stone tablet behind Rip Indra's boss room. Interact with it to receive your first dialogue from the Sealed King.

Next, travel to the Great Tree and climb to its summit. An invisible NPC called the Mysterious Force waits here—interact with them to be teleported to the Temple of Time. Pass through the temple doors, traverse the hallway, and exit. Return to the stone tablet for additional dialogue.

**Critical warning:** Do not skip speaking to the Sealed King a second time. Traveling directly to Mirage Island without this step will prevent the moon resonance from triggering correctly.

### 2. Moon Resonance and Blue Gear Collection

With your Mirror Fractal equipped, travel to Mirage Island during nighttime. Note that as of Update 19, a full moon is no longer required—any lunar phase works, though daytime remains incompatible.

Stand atop the highest peak of Mirage Island and activate your race's V3 ability while facing the moon. Upon success, you'll receive the message: "Your Mirror Fractal has resonated with the moon." A Blue Gear will then spawn somewhere on the island.

Search the island thoroughly and touch the Blue Gear before night ends. Its glowing blue appearance makes it visible from a distance, but you must collect it before sunrise.

### 3. Activating the Trial System

Return to the Temple of Time. Instead of proceeding forward toward the Mysterious Force, take the left hallway leading to an empty corridor. Look to the right wall and ascend—you'll discover a hidden lever that unlocks permanent trial access.

This is a one-time action. Once pulled, you never need to repeat these puzzle steps, even when switching to different races later.

## Entering Race Trials

Starting trials requires specific conditions:

- **Three or more players** must be present, each using a different race at V3 or higher
- All participants must have completed the Sealed King quest and obtained their Blue Gear
- A full moon must be active in the server

Wait for the full moon message to appear ("The light of a full moon peaks through the clouds"), then gather your team at their respective trial room doors. When all players simultaneously activate their race abilities, the doors will open.

### Post-Trial PvP Battle

After completing your trial, all participants must fight each other. Only the last player standing receives their gear. Use this opportunity to eliminate weakened opponents while conserving your V4 bar for crucial moments. Fruits like Dough, Buddha, or Blizzard perform exceptionally in these battles due to their crowd control capabilities.

The winner follows a glowing orb to the Ancient Clock, interacts with it, and places their first gear to unlock Race Awakening.

## V4 Abilities by Race

### Human V4: The Berserker

Human V4 transforms you into a damage-dealing machine. Your transformation grants +10% damage, speed, and healing alongside three Flash Step charges—one providing invisibility and another offering increased speed. The Limit Break passive lets your power scale with combat engagement, reaching up to 150% damage amplification. Moves cannot be interrupted mid-animation once upgraded to Tier 2.

**Best for:** Aggressive glass-cannon builds and burst damage PvP strategies.

### Shark V4: The Leviathan

Shark V4 emphasizes survivability through a regenerating water shield that refills as you deal damage. The shield displays as a secondary health bar that only restores through combat. Whirlpool applies stacking slow effects to enemies, making kiting and pursuit significantly easier.

**Best for:** Extended boss fights, grinding sessions, and defensive PvP playstyles.

### Mink V4: The Thunderbolt

Mink V4 excels in mobility. Dashing leaves tornadoes that trap and stun enemies temporarily, while Lightning Cloak extends dash distance dramatically. At Tier 2, holding the dash button triggers a "super dash" that covers enormous distances.

**Best for:** Hit-and-run tactics, evading multiple attackers, and navigating difficult terrain.

### Angel V4: The His Majesty

Angel V4 grants exceptional aerial control. Prince of the Skies allows gliding and eventually free flight by manipulating jump and dash inputs. King's Rule creates a light aura that applies slowness, damage-over-time, energy drain, and screen distortion effects to nearby enemies.

**Best for:** Aerial combat, supporting allies in team fights, and controlling battlefield zones.

### Ghoul V4: The Nightwalker

Ghoul V4 focuses on lifesteal and area denial. Domain Expansion creates a dark field reducing enemy regeneration, applying blindness, and reducing cooldowns. Blood Siphon grants approximately 25% life leech against players, making sustained fights highly favorable.

**Best for:** Prolonged engagements, vampire-style sustain builds, and night-themed strategies.

### Cyborg V4: The Genesis

Cyborg V4 combines defensive durability with energy-based offense. Energy Control chains damage to nearby enemies through orbs and enables powerful super jumps and super dashes. Aftershock temporarily disables opponent instinct abilities through an electrifying effect.

**Best for:** Explosive combo setups, disrupting enemy positioning, and sustained energy warfare.

## Maximizing Your Awakening

Completing the initial trial provides your first gear and basic Ancient Powers. However, full awakening requires additional trials, each growing progressively harder:

**Training costs accumulate to 26,750 Fragments per race for complete awakening.** The system requires roughly 77 minutes of transformation time per race across all training stages.

Key progression insights:

- **Four successful trials** unlock all abilities
- **Fifth trial** grants Ancient Clock access for gear swapping
- **Transformation duration** extends to approximately 1 minute 50 seconds when fully maxed
- **Awakening activation** uses Y key or left mouse button when the meter fills

## Choosing the Best Race for Your Playstyle

**PvE and Grinding Focus:**
Fishman and Cyborg V4 variants dominate extended fights. Fishman's regenerating shield sustains through boss mechanics, while Cyborg's chain damage clears mob waves efficiently.

**Competitive PvP:**
Human V4 delivers the raw power needed for burst combos. Its rage mechanic rewards aggressive play, and invisibility on Flash Step creates unpredictable engagement patterns.

**Mobile Playstyles:**
Mink V4's lightning-fast dashes and tornado traps excel when you need to evade multiple opponents or traverse the map quickly.

**Support and Control:**
Angel V4's aerial superiority and zone-control aura make it invaluable for team-based content.

## Tips for Success

**Finding Mirage Island:**
- Remain in Sea Danger Level 5 (Level 6 fog interferes with visibility)
- Bringing more players increases spawn chances
- Private servers can be used to wait for full moon cycles predictably

**Training Efficiency:**
- Dealing and receiving damage charges the awakening meter faster than passive methods
- Buddha fruit awakened form with fast melee styles allows indefinite transformation training
- Sea Beast combat charges awakening as quickly as player-versus-player encounters

**Trial Preparation:**
- Ensure your build supports both the trial mechanics and post-trial PvP
- Avoid high-knockback moves that can push opponents out of the arena
- Coordinate with teammates having different races to access trials simultaneously

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What level is required to unlock Race V4 Awakening?

You need access to the Third Sea, which requires reaching approximately level 1750. For Rip Indra specifically, level 2000 or higher is recommended due to the boss's difficulty.

### Do I need a specific fruit to unlock Race V4?

No fruit is required for unlocking Race V4. Any fruit or no fruit works for the trials. However, fruits like Dough, Buddha, or Blizzard provide significant advantages in the post-trial PvP battles.

### Can I complete Race V4 trials alone?

No. You need at least three players, each with different races at V3 or higher. All participants must stand at their respective trial doors and activate race abilities simultaneously during a full moon.

### How long does full Race V4 awakening take?

Complete awakening costs 26,750 Fragments and requires approximately 77 minutes of active transformation time across all training stages. Additional time is needed for finding Mirage Island and waiting for full moon cycles.

### Which Race V4 is best for PvP?

Human V4 excels for aggressive burst damage builds, while Mink V4 suits evasive playstyles. For team PvP, Angel V4 provides excellent support and zone control capabilities.

### What happens after completing all five trials?

The fifth trial grants access to the Ancient Clock, allowing you to swap between your unlocked gears freely whenever you rejoin or reset your character.
