## How to Read This List

Each item shows:
- **Trade Value** — community-agreed worth in the trading ecosystem
- **Rarity** — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, Mythical, or Limited/Gamepass

**Overpaid** tag means the item trades above its base value due to scarcity or hype. These can be risky to acquire.

## Understanding Demand and Status

**Demand Rating (1-10)** shows how many players are actively trying to acquire an item right now:
- **9-10**: Near-instant trades, everyone wants this
- **6-8**: Active market with reasonable trade times
- **3-5**: Moderate demand, may take time to find buyers
- **1-2**: Very slow market, hard to flip

**Status Tags**:
- **Stable** — value matches base desirability
- **Overpaid** — trades above base value due to scarcity or hype
- **Trending Up** — demand increasing, value rising
- **Trending Down** — demand decreasing

## Trading Tips

### Before You Trade

1. **Check the value list** before every trade — even if you think you know the value
2. **Demand matters as much as value** — a 10M item with 10/10 demand trades faster than a 50M item with 3/10 demand
3. **Verify Normal vs Permanent** — always confirm whether a fruit is regular or Permanent before agreeing
4. **Watch for trend shifts** — after updates, values can move fast

### Avoiding Scams

- Never trade based on what someone *claims* an item is worth
- Use community value lists as your baseline
- If a trade sounds "too good to be true," it probably is
- Always confirm the item name and type before accepting

## FAQ

### How are Blox Fruits trading values determined?

Values are set by community consensus from real player-to-player trades happening across major trading communities. Our list is updated daily based on monitoring active market activity.

### What does "Overpaid" mean?

"Overpaid" means the item trades above its base community value due to scarcity, hype, or recent buffs. Buying at overpaid values carries risk if demand later normalizes.

### Should I trade for Limited fruits?

Limited fruits like Galaxy Empyrean Kitsune have extreme trading value but very low liquidity. It can take significant time to flip a Limited at full value. Only trade for them if you plan to hold long-term.

### Why is Perm Dragon worth more than normal Dragon?

Permanent fruits cannot be re-rolled at the Blox Fruit Dealer, making them permanent inventory. This scarcity makes Perm versions worth 2-3x their normal counterparts.

### How often are values updated?

Values are reviewed and updated every single day. After major game updates or patch notes that buff or nerf a fruit, we push updated values within hours.
