## Blox Fruits Update 26 - Complete Guide

Update 26 launched on April 17, 2025, bringing major fruit reworks, new fruits, and a groundbreaking progression system. Here's everything you need to know.

## New Fruits

### Eagle Fruit (formerly Falcon)
The Falcon fruit has been completely reworked into the Eagle fruit with enhanced aerial combat abilities:
- Improved flight mechanics with faster movement
- New dive-bomb attack with increased damage
- Better combo potential in PvP
- A-tier ranking in our latest tier list

### Creation Fruit (formerly Barrier)
The Barrier fruit has been transformed into the Creation fruit:
- New offensive and defensive hybrid abilities
- Can create structures for both attack and defense
- Versatile playstyle that works in PvP and PvE
- A-tier ranking after the rework

## Reworked Fruits

### Gravity Rework
The Gravity fruit received a major update:
- Improved gravitational pull mechanics
- Better crowd control abilities
- Enhanced visual effects
- More viable in PvP combat

### Flame Rework
The Flame fruit was updated with:
- New fruit model and visual effects
- Improved damage output
- Better area-of-effect coverage
- More viable for grinding and PvP

## Fruit Progression System

The biggest addition in Update 26 is the new Fruit Progression System:

- **How it works**: A new upgrade system tied to fruit experimentation
- **Progression levels**: Each fruit can now be upgraded through use
- **Benefits**: Unlock new abilities, increased damage, and visual changes
- **Tips**: Focus on one fruit at a time to maximize progression

## Max Level Increase

The maximum level cap has been raised:
- **Previous**: Level 2600
- **New**: Level 2650
- New quests and areas available for levels 2600-2650
- End-game content has been adjusted accordingly

## Tips for Update 26

1. **Try the new Eagle fruit** - The rework makes it much more viable
2. **Experiment with Creation** - The hybrid playstyle is unique and powerful
3. **Focus on Fruit Progression** - Start leveling your favorite fruit early
4. **Re-evaluate your builds** - Gravity and Flame changes may shift the meta
5. **Use stat reset codes** - Take advantage of free resets to try new builds

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Update 26 free?
Yes, all Update 26 content is free. You just need to find or purchase the new fruits in-game.

### Do I keep my old Falcon/Barrier fruit?
Yes, they automatically convert to Eagle and Creation respectively with all your progress intact.

### How do I access the Fruit Progression System?
The system is available through the fruit menu. Select any fruit you own to see its progression options.
