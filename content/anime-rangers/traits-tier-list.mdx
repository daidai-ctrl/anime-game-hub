## What Are Traits in Anime Rangers X?

Traits are special passive modifiers that provide powerful stat boosts or unique effects to your units. Every unit in Anime Rangers X can have up to two traits: a **Main-Trait** (guaranteed on all units) and a **Sub-Trait** (extremely rare, with less than 1% chance of appearing alongside your main trait).

The right trait can transform an average unit into a battlefield powerhouse. Whether you're looking to increase damage, extend attack range, boost survivability, or reduce unit costs, traits offer strategic advantages that can carry you through the toughest stages in the game.

### Main-Traits vs. Sub-Traits

**Main-Traits** are the primary trait modifiers that every unit receives. These can be rerolled using Trait Reroll Shards. **Sub-Traits**, on the other hand, have an extremely low chance of appearing and provide additional minor stat boosts. Unlike Main-Traits, Sub-Traits cannot be directly rerolled—you'll need to reroll your Main-Trait and hope for a Sub-Trait to appear alongside it.

## S Tier Traits (Best in the Game)

The S-tier traits are the cream of the crop in Anime Rangers X. These rare and powerful modifiers can completely transform your units, making them essential for any serious player looking to dominate the battlefield.

### 1. Sovereign

**Rarity:** Mythic (0.1%) | **Effect:** +300% HP, +300% Damage, +50% Range, lowers spawn cap to 1

Sovereign is arguably the most powerful trait in Anime Rangers X. With a staggering **300% increase to both HP and Damage**, your unit becomes an unstoppable force capable of absorbing tremendous punishment while dealing devastating damage. The additional 50% range boost ensures your enhanced unit can strike from a safe distance.

The only drawback is the spawn cap reduction to 1, which makes this trait ideal for powerful single units rather than swarming strategies. Sovereign is the go-to choice for players focusing on elite singular units that can solo challenging content.

### 2. Duplicator

**Rarity:** Mythic (0.15%) | **Effect:** +100% HP, +100% Damage, spawns 2 units instead of 1

Duplicator offers an incredibly unique effect that no other trait matches—**your unit spawns twice instead of once**. This effectively doubles your unit's field presence for the cost of one placement. Combined with the 100% boosts to HP and Damage, Duplicator is absolutely essential for players who want maximum value from their unit placements.

This trait excels on units with high placement caps, buff providers, and crowd control specialists. Meta support units like Griffin, Omega Dragonlord, and Koromu become even more valuable with Duplicator equipped.

### 3. Capitalist

**Rarity:** Mythic (0.25%) | **Effect:** -50% Cost, +50% Damage, +50% HP, +1 spawn cap

Capitalist provides an unbeatable combination of **massive cost reduction and powerful stat boosts**. The -50% unit cost reduction allows you to summon and upgrade expensive late-game units at half the price, while the +50% Damage and HP ensure your investment pays dividends in combat.

The +1 spawn cap increase means you can field an additional unit, making Capitalist perfect for players who want to maximize their team composition efficiency. This trait is especially valuable for summoning Mythic and Secret tier units that would otherwise break your budget.

### 4. Seraph

**Rarity:** Mythic (0.5%) | **Effect:** +50% Range, +50% Damage, +50% HP, +30% Evade

Seraph delivers **balanced boosts across all key combat stats** while adding a valuable 30% Evade chance. This makes it an excellent all-around trait that works well on nearly any unit in your roster.

The Evade chance is particularly valuable for frontline units that take frequent damage, giving them much-needed survivability against boss attacks and enemy waves. Seraph is easier to obtain than the other Mythic traits, making it a more accessible option for players who want top-tier performance without the extreme luck required for Sovereign or Duplicator.

## How to Reroll Traits in Anime Rangers X

Rerolling traits is essential for obtaining better modifiers for your units. Here's how the trait reroll system works:

### Step-by-Step Reroll Guide

1. **Collect Trait Reroll Shards:** Gather reroll currency through:
   - Completing daily quests and missions
   - Using active redeem codes (check our Anime Rangers X codes guide)
   - Purchasing from in-game merchants
   - Evolving units
   - Direct purchase from the in-game shop

2. **Visit the Upgrade Building:** Head to the main lobby and locate the Upgrade building.

3. **Talk to the Trait NPC:** Inside the Upgrade building, find and interact with the Trait NPC.

4. **Select Your Unit:** Choose the unit you wish to modify.

5. **Confirm the Reroll:** Spend your Trait Reroll Shards to replace the unit's current trait with a new random one.

### Reroll Tips

- **Be Patient:** Traits are random, so expect to reroll many times to get a top-tier trait
- **Focus on Meta Units:** Only use Trait Reroll Shards on S-tier and meta units—wasting them on weak early-game units is inefficient
- **Save for Events:** Occasionally, the game offers increased drop rates for rare traits during special events
- **Consider Sub-Traits:** While sub-traits are rare, they provide additional minor boosts. If you roll a good main trait with a sub-trait, consider keeping it even if the main isn't perfect

## FAQ: Anime Rangers X Traits

### What is the best trait in Anime Rangers X?

**Sovereign** is widely considered the best trait in Anime Rangers X due to its massive +300% HP and +300% Damage boosts. However, **Duplicator** is equally valuable for its unique ability to spawn two units instead of one. The best trait ultimately depends on your playstyle and team composition.

### Can you change traits in Anime Rangers X?

Yes, you can reroll traits using Trait Reroll Shards. Visit the Upgrade building in the main lobby, interact with the Trait NPC, select your unit, and spend reroll currency to obtain a new random trait.

### How rare is the Sovereign trait?

Sovereign has a Mythic rarity with approximately a **0.1% chance** of appearing when rerolling. This makes it one of the rarest traits in the game, alongside Duplicator (0.15%).

### What traits work best for support units?

For meta support units like **Griffin**, **Omega Dragonlord**, and **Koromu**, the best traits are:
1. **Duplicator** – Doubles your support presence
2. **Capitalist** – Reduces costs while boosting stats
3. **Sovereign** – Maximizes individual unit power

### Do sub-traits stack with main traits?

Yes, sub-traits provide additional stat boosts that stack with your main trait's effects. However, sub-traits have less than 1% chance of appearing and cannot be directly rerolled.
