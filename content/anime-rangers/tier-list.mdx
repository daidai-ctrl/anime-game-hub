## Anime Rangers X Tier List - May 2026

Our comprehensive tier list ranks every unit in Anime Rangers X based on their overall performance in battles, crowd control, and area coverage.

The absolute best units in the game:

- **Solar Fist** - Top-tier DPS, can carry through all content
- **Gojo** - Overpowered abilities and defense
- **Goku Ultra Instinct** - Massive damage output

## How to Build the Best Team

1. **Start with one S-tier DPS** - Solar Fist or Goku UI as your carry
2. **Add crowd control** - Luffy Gear 5 or Gojo for AoE
3. **Include a tank** - Units that can absorb damage
4. **Fill with support** - Buff and heal units to sustain your team
5. **Use Trait Rerolls wisely** - Focus on your S-tier units first
