## Anime Rangers X Best Teams Guide

Team composition is everything in Anime Rangers X. The right team can clear content effortlessly, while a poorly built team will struggle. This guide covers the best meta teams for every situation.

## Best Meta Teams

### Team 1: Burst Damage Team
| Slot | Character | Role |
|------|-----------|------|
| 1 | Solar Fist | Main DPS |
| 2 | Madara | Sub DPS + CC |
| 3 | Gojo | Support + Shield |
| 4 | Tanjiro | Healer |

**Strategy**: Open with Gojo's shield, use Madara's CC to lock enemies, then Solar Fist burst to finish.

### Team 2: Sustain Team
| Slot | Character | Role |
|------|-----------|------|
| 1 | Levi | Main DPS |
| 2 | Naruto Baryon | Sub DPS |
| 3 | Orihime | Healer |
| 4 | All Might | Tank |

**Strategy**: Slow and steady. All Might absorbs damage while Levi and Naruto chip away.

### Team 3: Speed Clear Team
| Slot | Character | Role |
|------|-----------|------|
| 1 | Goku UI | Main DPS |
| 2 | Vegeta UE | Sub DPS |
| 3 | Luffy Gear 5 | AoE Clear |
| 4 | Ichigo | Flex |

**Strategy**: Maximum damage output. Clear waves before they can react.

## Team Building Principles

1. **Balance roles** - Every team needs DPS, Support, and Tank/Healer
2. **Synergy matters** - Characters that buff each other's damage are worth more than raw stats
3. **Element coverage** - Cover at least 3 elements to handle any boss
4. **Cost efficiency** - Don't overspend on one slot at the expense of others

## Free-to-Play Team

If you're F2P, focus on:
- **Tanjiro** (free from story)
- **Naruto** (free from login rewards)
- **Ichigo** (easily farmable)
- **Luffy** (event reward)

This team can clear most content with proper strategy and upgrades.
