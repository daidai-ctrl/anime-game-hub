Anime Rangers X (officially renamed **Re Rangers X**) is a Roblox tower defense game that brings together legendary anime heroes to defend the multiverse from waves of enemies. Unlike traditional tower defense, your units move and actively engage approaching enemies, making team composition and strategy crucial.

## Game Basics

### How to Start

1. Search and install Anime Rangers X on Roblox
2. Join the official Learning Disability Group (required to redeem codes)
3. Complete the in-game tutorial — it guides you through basic operations
4. Choose one of three starter units (see below)
5. Reach **Level 5** to unlock code redemption

### Core Mechanics

- **Units** — anime-inspired characters you summon and deploy to defend against enemies
- **Summons** — spend Gems (50 per summon) at the Summon portal for new units
- **Rarities** — Common → Epic → Legendary → Mythic → Secret → Ranger → S+ (God tier)
- **Traits** — permanent stat bonuses applied to units via reroll shards
- **Evolutions** — upgrade units to their final forms, unlocking new abilities and stat boosts
- **Limit Breaks** — increase max level from 50 to 100 using Dr. Megga Punk items

## Best Starter Units

When you launch the game for the first time, you pick one of three starter units. **Leaf Ninja (Naruto)** is the best choice for beginners.

| Starter | Why Pick It |
|---------|-------------|
| **Leaf Ninja** | Best pick. High movement speed, rapid attack skill for wave clearing, evasion chance for survivability. Easy to spawn multiple copies early. |
| Rubber Pirate | Decent damage, water-based mobility |
| Saiyan Warrior | Basic powerhouse, straightforward but less versatile |

Leaf Ninja's high base speed lets you breeze through early stages, and its evasion passive grants extra survivability in PvE situations. You can spawn multiple Narutos in early summons to steamroll through the first few chapters.

## Quick Progression Roadmap

### Early Game (Level 1-15)

- Focus on clearing Story Mode stages as fast as possible
- Use all your Gems on summons to build roster variety
- Redeem all active codes (see below)
- Join an active guild for shared buffs and tips
- Start farming AFK Chambers for passive rewards
- Unlock Boss Rush at Level 15 and prioritize it for better rewards

### Mid Game (Level 15-50)

- Start focusing on **Trait rerolling** — apply reroll shards to your strongest units first
- Prioritize Mythic and Secret units over Legendary for upgrades
- Begin farming **Limit Break** materials in Challenge Tower
- Work on **Evolution** materials for your core units
- Check our [Anime Rangers Tier List](/anime-rangers/tier-list) for the best units to invest in

### Late Game (Level 50+)

- Unlock the trading system (available at Level 50+)
- Start trading for unobtainable units
- Focus on maxing Limit Breaks (level 100 cap)
- Push into high-wave content and raids
- Optimize trait rolls — aim for Superior, Horizon, or rare Seraph/Capitalist traits

## Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Rerolling traits on starter units** — save reroll shards for your best Mythic and Secret units
2. **Spreading resources too thin** — focus upgrades on 3-5 core units rather than upgrading everything evenly
3. **Ignoring Elemental Tower** — it provides permanent stat boosts that stack over time
4. **Not joining a guild** — guild buffs and weekly bosses provide significant advantages
5. **Skipping events** — event-exclusive units and rewards are often unobtainable elsewhere
6. **Spending Gems on cosmetic bundles** — always prioritize summons for new units first
7. **Forgetting to use codes** — new codes drop frequently with free rerolls, gems, and resources

## FAQ

### How do I start Anime Rangers X?

Search for "Re Rangers X" or "Anime Rangers X" on Roblox, install the game, complete the tutorial, and pick Leaf Ninja as your starter unit. Reach Level 5 to unlock code redemption.

### What are the best units for beginners?

Leaf Ninja (starter), Fire Fist, Solar Fist, Eclipse Sorcerer, and Demon Slayer are the best early-game picks. Focus on building a roster of Mythic units before chasing Secret rarity.

### How do I redeem codes?

Open the game, click the Code button on the left side of the screen, enter your code, and click Redeem. Most codes require Level 5 minimum, and some require Level 15-20.

### How do I get Gems quickly?

Complete Story Mode stages, talk to every NPC (first conversations give free Gems), interact with buildings like the Evolve area, complete quests, and participate in events. Redeem all active codes for bonus Gems.

### Should I reroll my unit traits?

Yes, but only on your best units. Apply reroll shards to Mythic and Secret units first. Aim for Superior (damage boost), Horizon (range), or rare traits like Sovereign, Capitalist, and Seraph.

### What is the trading system?

Trading unlocks at Level 50. It lets you exchange units with other players. S+ tier and God tier units are often only obtainable through trading. Join active trading communities to find deals.
