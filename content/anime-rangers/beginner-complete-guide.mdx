## What Is Re:Rangers X?

Re:Rangers X is an anime tower defense / action RPG hybrid developed by LD Productions. Unlike traditional TD games where you place static towers, you summon anime-inspired units that automatically march across the battlefield, engaging enemies in real-time combat. The game features six rarity tiers: **Common → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Mythic → Secret**, with units inspired by popular anime franchises.

The game received a major overhaul in February 2026 and launched **Update 3.0 "The Bounty Hunt"** on April 1st, introducing the new Soul Tier system, a One Piece-themed event, and the Strongest Man unit. Four currencies drive progression: **Gems, Yin, Gold, and Trait Rerolls**, plus endgame currencies like Cursed Scrolls and Soul Fragments.

## Core Progression Systems

### Traits System

Traits add permanent stat bonuses to your units. You can reroll traits using **Trait Reroll Shards**, obtainable through daily quests, redeemable codes, the Merchant NPC, and standard gameplay.

**Trait Tiers (Best to Worst):**

| Tier | Traits | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Mythical** | Sovereign (+300%), Duplicator (20% spawn copy), Capitalist (-50% cost), Seraph (+50% all stats) | Game-changing bonuses |
| **Rare** | Blitz (-20% SPA), Fortune (+20% Cash/-10% cost) | Solid utility |
| **Uncommon** | Marksman (+30% Range), Scholar (+50% XP) | Helpful early |
| **Common** | Vigor/Swift/Range (+5-15% stats) | Minor bonuses |

**Pro Tip:** Save reroll shards for your Mythic and Secret units. A Capitalist trait on Shadow Monarch can cut your upgrade costs by half.

### Evolution System

Evolution transforms units into their stronger final forms, unlocking new abilities and stat increases. The most important evolution path is **King of Shadows → Shadow Monarch**, which represents the single largest power spike in the game.

- Each evolution requires specific materials earned from Ranger stages or purchased with Yin
- Higher rarity characters require more materials
- Start farming evolution materials early—you'll need them for your core units

### Limit Break System

Limit Breaks increase a unit's max level from 50 to 100. Visit **Dr. Megga Punk** (the white-coated NPC near the Traits Tower) to trade two items for each +5 level boost. Farm these items in the Challenge Tower or purchase from the Speed Cart Merchant.

### Soul Tier System (New in Update 3.0)

The new Soul Tier system adds another layer of progression for endgame players. Collect Soul Fragments through Bounty Hunt mode and special events to unlock additional stat bonuses beyond Level 100.

## Quick Progression Roadmap

### Early Game (Level 1-15)
- Pick **Leaf Ninja** as your starter
- Redeem all active codes immediately
- Focus 70% of your Yin on your top 3 units—don't spread resources thin
- Rush Shadow Monarch evolution (King of Shadows → Shadow Monarch)
- Clear Story Mode stages for Gems
- Unlock Boss Rush at Level 15 and prioritize it

### Mid Game (Level 15-50)
- Start **Trait rerolling** on your strongest Mythic/Secret units
- Work on Evolution materials for core units
- Farm Limit Break materials in Challenge Tower
- Build around Radio Girl as your primary support
- Begin farming Bounty Hunt for Soul Fragments

### Late Game (Level 50+)
- Unlock trading system and join trading communities
- Focus on maxing Limit Breaks (Level 100 cap)
- Optimize trait rolls—aim for Sovereign, Capitalist, or Duplicator
- Push high-wave Infinite content and raids
- Collect Soul Tier upgrades for endgame power

## Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Rerolling traits on starter units** — Save reroll shards exclusively for Mythic and Secret units
2. **Spreading resources across too many units** — Three strong units > six mediocre ones
3. **Ignoring elemental advantages** — Fire beats Leaf, Water beats Fire, Lightning beats Water
4. **Not redeeming codes early** — Missing free Gems and rerolls sets you back significantly
5. **Skipping Boss Rush** — Limit Break materials are essential for endgame power
6. **Forgetting Soul Fragments** — New Update 3.0 resource that's crucial for max-level players

## FAQ

### What is the best starter unit in Re:Rangers X?

**Leaf Ninja (Naruto)** is the best starter. It has high movement speed for early stage clearing, rapid attack skills, and an evasion passive for survivability. Multiple Narutos can be spawned early to steamroll through first chapters.

### How do I get Shadow Monarch in Re:Rangers X?

Shadow Monarch is the evolved form of **King of Shadows**. You'll need duplicate King of Shadows copies and specific evolution materials from Boss Rush stages. This evolution represents the single largest power spike in the game.

### What are the best support units?

**Radio Girl** is currently the best support unit, providing team-wide damage buffs and enemy defense reduction. **Fairy Queen** offers excellent healing and team buffs. Support units often define the meta more than raw DPS units.

### How do I earn Gems quickly?

Complete Story Mode stages (~15 gems per clear), redeem all active codes, interact with NPCs (first conversations give free Gems), complete Player Quests, and participate in limited-time events.

### What is Soul Tier in Update 3.0?

Soul Tier is a new endgame progression system introduced in Update 3.0 "The Bounty Hunt." Collect Soul Fragments through Bounty Hunt mode to unlock additional permanent stat bonuses beyond Level 100.

### Is Re:Rangers X free to play?

Yes, the game is free to play on Roblox. You can progress through all content without spending Robux, though paid players can accelerate their progression with cosmetic bundles and convenience items.
