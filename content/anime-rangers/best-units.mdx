## Best Units in Anime Rangers X (May 2026)

Choosing the right units in Anime Rangers X can make the difference between clearing a stage effortlessly or struggling through every wave. With over 50 units available, knowing which ones to invest your gems and resources into is crucial. This guide covers the absolute best units for every game mode.

## S-Tier Units - The Absolute Best

### Solar Fist
Solar Fist remains the king of AoE damage in Anime Rangers X. His ultimate ability "Solar Flare Blast" hits all enemies on the screen for massive damage, making him indispensable for wave clearing and boss fights alike. At max upgrade, his attack speed increases by 40%, allowing near-constant ability uptime.

**Best For:** PvE stages, Boss fights, Wave clearing

### Madara (Eternal)
The Eternal version of Madara is a limited-time unit that dominated every game mode. His "Meteor Strike" ability deals devastating single-target damage while also providing crowd control. If you were lucky enough to pull him during the event, he's worth every upgrade.

**Best For:** PvP, Boss fights, Single-target DPS

### Gear 5 Hero
Based on the iconic transformation, Gear 5 Hero brings incredible versatility. His rubber-based attacks have unique hitboxes that can reach enemies others can't, and his awakening form provides a massive stat boost.

**Best For:** All game modes, Versatile team composition

## A-Tier Units - Excellent Choices

### Blue Emperor
Blue Emperor excels in sustained DPS with his ice-based attacks that slow enemies. At upgrade level 5, he gains a passive that increases team damage by 15%, making him a great team player.

**Best For:** Team support, Sustained DPS, Slow comps

### Crimson Blade
A pure damage dealer with incredible single-target burst. Crimson Blade can one-shot most non-boss enemies with his critical hit ability. The trade-off is his relatively low health pool.

**Best For:** PvP burst, Boss damage, Glass cannon builds

### Shadow Monarch
Shadow Monarch summons shadow soldiers that fight alongside him, effectively multiplying your team's damage output. His shadows inherit a percentage of his stats, scaling incredibly well with upgrades.

**Best For:** PvE farming, Multi-target encounters, Solo carry

## B-Tier Units - Solid Performers

### Flame Emperor
Solid AoE damage with fire-based attacks that leave burning damage over time. Good for stages with many enemies but outclassed by Solar Fist in most situations.

### Thunder God
Fast attack speed with chain lightning that bounces between enemies. Excellent for grouped enemies but struggles against spread-out formations.

### Wind Dancer
Support unit that increases team movement speed and dodge chance. Valuable in PvP but less impactful in PvE where positioning is less critical.

## FAQ

### What is the best unit in Anime Rangers X?
Solar Fist is generally considered the best overall unit due to his unmatched AoE damage and versatility across all game modes.

### Should I reroll for Madara (Eternal)?
Madara (Eternal) is a limited unit and no longer available. If you missed him, focus on Solar Fist or Gear 5 Hero instead.

### Can F2P players compete in PvP?
Yes, with units like Thunder God and Wind Dancer from free sources, you can build competitive teams. It takes longer but is definitely possible.

### How often are new units added?
New units are typically added every 2-3 weeks through update patches, with major unit releases coinciding with game updates.
