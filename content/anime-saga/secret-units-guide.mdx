## Tips for Getting Secret Heroes

1. **Save Gems** — Don't spend on normal banners, wait for secret rate-up events
2. **Complete Challenges** — Some secrets are locked behind specific achievements
3. **Participate in Events** — Limited-time events are the only way to get certain secrets
4. **Join a Guild** — Guild rewards sometimes include secret heroe fragments

## Are Secret Heroes Worth It?

Absolutely. Secret heroes in Anime Saga are significantly stronger than even evolved S-tier heroes. If you're serious about endgame content, obtaining at least one secret heroe should be a priority.
