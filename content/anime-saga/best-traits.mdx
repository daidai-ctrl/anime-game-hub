## Understanding Traits in Anime Saga

The trait system in Anime Saga allows you to customize your heroes with passive bonuses that enhance their performance. Choosing the right trait can dramatically improve effectiveness in combat, farming, and PvP.

## How to Get Good Traits

1. **Trait Reroll** — Use Gems or special items to reroll traits
2. **Farming** — Some game modes drop trait enhancement items
3. **Events** — Limited-time events often reward premium trait items
4. **Evolution Bonuses** — Some evolutions unlock exclusive traits

## Trait Priority Guide

- **DPS Heroes** — Prioritize damage-boosting traits like Legendary or Celestial
- **Support Heroes** — Look for healing or buff-enhancing traits
- **Hybrid Heroes** — Balanced traits that improve both offense and defense

**What is the best trait in Anime Saga?**
Legendary is generally considered the best trait in Anime Saga for 2026 due to its strong damage and speed bonuses.
