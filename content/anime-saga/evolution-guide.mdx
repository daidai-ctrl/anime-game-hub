## Anime Saga Evolution Guide (2026) — All Evolutions & Materials

Evolution is the most powerful upgrade system in Anime Saga. Evolved heroes gain massive stat boosts and new abilities, making them significantly stronger than their base forms.

## All Evolutions in Anime Saga

| Base | Evolved | Materials | Bonus |
|------|---------|-----------|-------|
| Goku | **Goku Ultra Instinct** | 10 Spirit Emblems + 5 Kaioken Cores | 2x Damage, Ultra Instinct Skill |
| Naruto | **Naruto Baryon Mode** | 15 Sage Essences + 8 Kurama Shards | 3x Damage, Baryon Skill |
| Luffy | **Luffy Gear 5** | 8 Haki Stones + 4 Gum Shards | 2x Speed, Gear 5 Transformation |
| Vegeta | **Vegeta Evolved** | 12 Pride Shards + 6 Saiyan Crystals | 2.5x Damage, Final Flash Skill |
| Deku | **Deku Full Cowl 100%** | 6 One For All Shards + 3 Hero Crystals | 2x Crit, Full Cowl Skill |

## Evolution Priority

1. **Goku → Goku Ultra Instinct** — Highest damage increase, essential for endgame
2. **Naruto → Naruto Baryon Mode** — Massive AoE upgrade, great for farming
3. **Luffy → Luffy Gear 5** — Strong PvP evolution with new skills

## How to Farm Evolution Materials

- **Daily Dungeons** — Consistent source of evolution materials
- **Raid Bosses** — Higher drop rates for rare materials
- **Events** — Limited-time events often provide evolution items
- **Shop** — Some materials can be purchased with Gold

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which heroe should I evolve first?**
Evolve Goku first — the Goku Ultra Instinct form is a massive upgrade that carries in all content.

**Can I undo an evolution?**
No, evolutions are permanent. Make sure you want to evolve before committing the materials.
