## Anime Story 2 Farming Guide

Efficient farming is the backbone of progression in Anime Story 2. Whether you need gems for summoning, upgrade materials for your units, or XP to level up, knowing where and how to farm makes the difference between slow grinding and fast advancement. This guide details the best stages, optimal farming routes, and daily routines for maximum resource gain.

### Best Gem Farming Stages

Gems are the most valuable currency in Anime Story 2. They are used for summoning new units and purchasing event items. Here are the best stages for gem farming:

- **Chapter 7-3 (Spirit Academy)** — Fastest clear time at 45 seconds with 15-20 gems per run. Use a speed-focused team with Shadow Blade and Flame Archer. This is your go-to stage for gem grinding.
- **Chapter 15-5 (Dark Forest)** — Yields 25-35 gems per run but takes 90 seconds. Better gem-per-run ratio but slower overall if you can clear 7-3 quickly.
- **Daily Quests** — Complete all daily quests every day for a guaranteed 200-300 gems. This is non-negotiable for consistent income.
- **Weekly Boss Challenge** — Defeating the weekly boss grants 500 gems plus rare materials. Reset happens every Monday.

### Best Upgrade Material Farming

Upgrade materials come in three tiers: Common, Rare, and Epic. Different stages drop different materials:

- **Common Materials (Tier 1-4 upgrades)** — Farm Chapters 3-1 through 5-10 in Spirit Academy. These stages have high drop rates and quick clear times. Each run takes 30 seconds and drops 3-5 common materials.
- **Rare Materials (Tier 5-8 upgrades)** — Farm Chapters 12-1 through 15-10 in Dark Forest. These stages are harder but drop rare materials consistently. Bring a tank and healer for safe farming.
- **Epic Materials (Tier 9+ upgrades)** — Farm Chapter 20+ in Demon Castle or participate in weekend material events. Epic materials have low drop rates, so patience and persistence are required.

Pro tip: Focus on one unit at a time when farming materials. Spreading materials across multiple units slows everyone down.

### Best XP Farming Stages

Leveling units requires XP books dropped from specific stages:

- **Chapter 5-8 (Spirit Academy)** — Best XP-per-energy ratio for low-level units. Drops Small and Medium XP books.
- **Chapter 18-3 (Dark Forest)** — Best for mid-level units. Drops Medium and Large XP books.
- **Chapter 30-5 (Demon Castle)** — Endgame XP farming. Drops Large and Epic XP books. Requires a strong team to clear consistently.
- **XP Event Stages** — Available during weekend events. Double XP book drops make this the most efficient time to level units.

### Best Unit Shard Farming

Unit shards are needed to unlock and upgrade specific units. Shard farming is targeted — each stage drops shards for a specific unit:

- **Spirit Knight Shards** — Chapter 8 Boss (Spirit Academy), 2-3 shards per clear
- **Shadow Blade Shards** — Chapter 16 Boss (Dark Forest), 2-3 shards per clear
- **Celestial Healer Shards** — Chapter 22 Boss (Demon Castle), 1-2 shards per clear
- **Event-Exclusive Shards** — Only available during limited-time events. Always prioritize event shard farming since these are time-limited.

### Optimal Daily Farming Routine

Follow this daily schedule for maximum efficiency:

1. **Redeem all active codes** for free gems and items
2. **Complete all daily quests** for guaranteed gem income
3. **Farm gem stages** (Chapter 7-3) until energy is at 50%
4. **Farm material stages** based on your current upgrade needs
5. **Farm shard stages** for the unit you are currently building
6. **Use remaining energy** on XP farming stages
7. **Check event shop** for any limited-time purchases

### Energy Management Tips

Energy is your most limited resource. Here is how to maximize it:

- **Never let energy cap** — Log in regularly to spend energy before it reaches maximum
- **Use energy refills wisely** — Save refills for double-drop events
- **Prioritize events** — Event stages usually have better rewards per energy than permanent stages
- **Skip low-priority farming** — If you do not need common materials, skip those stages entirely

### Farming Mistakes to Avoid

- **Farming too many different materials at once** — Focus on one unit's upgrade path at a time
- **Ignoring daily quests** — These are the most efficient gem source in the game
- **Skipping events** — Limited-time events offer the best rewards per energy spent
- **Not using codes** — Free resources from codes add up significantly over time

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the fastest way to get gems in Anime Story 2?**
Farm Chapter 7-3 repeatedly while completing all daily quests. Using codes from our Codes page provides additional free gems instantly.

**How much energy should I spend on farming each day?**
Spend all of it. Energy that sits unused is wasted progression. Log in multiple times per day if needed to prevent capping.

**Should I farm XP or materials first?**
Farm materials first if your unit is at a tier upgrade threshold. Otherwise, farm XP to increase base stats for easier stage clears.

**Are event stages worth the energy cost?**
Always. Event stages have significantly better drop rates than permanent stages and often reward items unavailable elsewhere.
