## Anime Story 2 Best Traits

Traits are passive abilities that define how your units perform in battle. Choosing the right traits can transform an average unit into a top-tier powerhouse. This guide ranks every trait in Anime Story 2 and explains which combinations work best for each role.

### How Traits Work

Each unit can equip up to two traits. Traits provide passive bonuses such as increased damage, faster cooldowns, enhanced healing, or improved survivability. Traits are unlocked through story progression, boss drops, and event rewards. Higher-rarity traits offer stronger bonuses but are harder to obtain.

### S-Tier Traits — Must Have

**Critical Strike** — Increases critical hit rate by 25% and critical damage by 50%. The single best DPS trait in the game. Every damage-dealing unit benefits from this trait. Pairs perfectly with Shadow Blade's backstab ability for devastating critical hits.

**Fortress** — Reduces all incoming damage by 30% and grants a 15% chance to block attacks entirely. The ultimate tank trait. Spirit Knight with Fortress becomes nearly unkillable in story content and very hard to take down in PvP.

**Divine Blessing** — Increases healing output by 40% and adds a regeneration effect to all heals. Essential for any support unit. Celestial Healer with Divine Blessing can solo-heal most raid content.

### A-Tier Traits — Excellent Choices

**Swift Strike** — Reduces ability cooldowns by 20%. Great for units with powerful but slow abilities. Flame Archer benefits enormously since his ultimate can be used more frequently.

**Elemental Mastery** — Increases elemental damage by 35% and adds a chance to apply elemental status effects. Best on units with strong elemental attacks like Frost Mage or Flame Archer.

**Vampiric** — Heals the unit for 15% of damage dealt. Excellent for DPS units in solo content or when healing support is limited. Shadow Blade with Vampiric becomes self-sustaining in story stages.

**Guardian Aura** — Reduces damage taken by all allies within range by 15%. A solid team-oriented tank trait that stacks well with Fortress for maximum damage reduction.

### B-Tier Traits — Good But Situational

**Rapid Assault** — Increases attack speed by 20% but reduces damage per hit by 10%. Good for units with on-hit effects but reduces overall DPS for most units.

**Nature's Shield** — Grants a damage-absorbing shield at the start of each battle. Useful in PvP for surviving burst damage, but the shield does not scale well into endgame content.

**Precision** — Increases accuracy by 30%, ensuring attacks rarely miss. Helpful against evasion-focused enemies in later worlds, but unnecessary for most content.

**Resilience** — Reduces status effect duration by 40%. Niche but valuable against specific bosses that rely on stuns and freezes.

### C-Tier Traits — Below Average

**Lucky Find** — Increases drop rates by 10%. Only useful for farming, not combat. Switch to a combat trait before entering PvP or raids.

**Minor Recovery** — Heals 2% HP per turn. The healing is too small to matter in most fights. Divine Blessing is vastly superior for any support unit.

**Basic Fortitude** — Increases HP by 10%. A small boost that does not compare to the damage reduction from Fortress. Only use if you have no better tank trait available.

### Best Trait Combinations by Role

**DPS Units** — Critical Strike + Vampiric (sustain DPS) or Critical Strike + Elemental Mastery (maximum burst). Critical Strike is mandatory; the second slot depends on whether you need survival or raw damage.

**Tank Units** — Fortress + Guardian Aura (maximum team protection) or Fortress + Resilience (anti-status effect tank). Fortress is non-negotiable for tanks.

**Support Units** — Divine Blessing + Swift Strike (maximum healing frequency) or Divine Blessing + Guardian Aura (healing plus team damage reduction). Divine Blessing is the core support trait.

### How to Unlock Rare Traits

- **Story Completion** — S-Tier traits like Critical Strike are rewarded for completing specific story milestones
- **Boss Drops** — Fortress and Divine Blessing drop from Demon Castle bosses
- **Event Rewards** — Limited-time events often offer exclusive traits not available elsewhere
- **Trait Summoning** — Spend trait tokens for a chance to pull random rare traits
- **Shard Collection** — Collect enough trait shards to craft the trait directly

### Trait Upgrade Priority

Upgrade your traits in this order: Critical Strike > Fortress > Divine Blessing > Swift Strike > Elemental Mastery. Upgrading traits increases their bonus percentages and unlocks additional effects at higher levels.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can a unit have two of the same trait?**
No. Each trait can only be equipped once per unit. You must choose two different traits.

**What is the best trait for Shadow Blade?**
Critical Strike paired with Vampiric for sustain or Elemental Mastery for maximum burst damage. Critical Strike is always the first choice.

**How do I get S-Tier traits?**
Complete story milestones for Critical Strike, farm Demon Castle bosses for Fortress, and participate in events for Divine Blessing. Check our Codes page for any trait-related rewards.

**Should I upgrade all traits evenly?**
No. Focus on upgrading Critical Strike and Fortress first since they benefit the widest range of units. Divine Blessing is the next priority if you use healers frequently.
