<h2>Should You Reroll After an Update?</h2>
<p>You do not always need to reroll after every Anime Story 2 update. Rerolling is only worth considering if a new top-tier unit becomes available, if your current account is still early in progression, or if the update changes the value of beginner-friendly units.</p>

<p>If you already have several strong units, it is usually better to improve your current team instead of starting over. New players should compare the latest units before deciding whether rerolling is worth the time.</p>

<h2>Update Tips for Beginners</h2>
<p>Beginner players should avoid spending all resources immediately after an update. Wait until the community tests new units and traits, then decide which characters deserve upgrades. Early mistakes can slow down progression, especially if materials are hard to farm.</p>

<p>A safe beginner approach is to build a balanced team with one strong damage unit, one support unit, and one durable unit. Then upgrade slowly based on the latest tier list and farming requirements.</p>
