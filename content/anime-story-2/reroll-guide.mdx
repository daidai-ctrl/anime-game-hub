<h2>Anime Story 2 Reroll Guide</h2>
<p>Rerolling in Anime Story 2 can help new players start with stronger units, but it is not always necessary. This guide explains when rerolling is worth it, which types of units to aim for, and when it is better to continue progressing with your current account.</p>

<h2>Is Rerolling Worth It?</h2>
<p>Rerolling is most useful for brand-new players who want a stronger early start. If you already have upgraded units, unlocked progression, or collected important resources, rerolling may not be worth losing that progress.</p>

<p>Before rerolling, check the current tier list and best units guide to see whether your current account already has good options.</p>

<h2>Best Units to Reroll For</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Dragon Emperor:</strong> Strong carry potential and excellent damage.</li>
  <li><strong>Celestial Knight:</strong> Reliable frontline unit for progression.</li>
  <li><strong>Phoenix Healer:</strong> Valuable support option for harder fights.</li>
  <li><strong>Shadow Assassin:</strong> Strong burst damage for aggressive teams.</li>
  <li><strong>Thunder Lord:</strong> Good area damage and stage-clearing value.</li>
</ul>

<h2>When You Should Not Reroll</h2>
<ul>
  <li>You already have one or two strong units.</li>
  <li>You have progressed far into the game.</li>
  <li>You have used limited-time rewards or codes.</li>
  <li>You do not want to repeat early farming.</li>
  <li>Your team is good enough to clear current content.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Reroll Tips for Beginners</h2>
<p>New players should avoid rerolling endlessly. A perfect start is not required to enjoy Anime Story 2. It is usually better to get a strong enough unit, then start progressing and collecting resources.</p>

<p>If you are rerolling, decide your target before you begin. This prevents wasting time chasing every possible high-tier unit.</p>

<h2>Use Codes Before Rerolling</h2>

<h3>Should beginners reroll in Anime Story 2?</h3>
<p>Beginners can reroll if they want a stronger start, but it is not required. A good team can still be built through progression.</p>

<h3>What unit should I reroll for?</h3>
<p>Dragon Emperor, Celestial Knight, Phoenix Healer, Shadow Assassin, and Thunder Lord are strong targets depending on your preferred team style.</p>

<h3>Is rerolling better than farming?</h3>
<p>Rerolling helps at the start, but farming and upgrading are more important for long-term progression.</p>
