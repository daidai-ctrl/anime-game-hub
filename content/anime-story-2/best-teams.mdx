## Anime Story 2 Best Teams

Building the right team is the key to success in Anime Story 2. This guide covers the most effective team compositions for every game mode, including story progression, PvP arena, cooperative raids, and boss fights. Each composition is tested and verified for the current meta.

### Team Building Fundamentals

Every strong team in Anime Story 2 needs three core roles filled:

- **Main DPS** — Deals the highest damage and carries the team through fights
- **Tank** — Absorbs enemy attacks and protects weaker units
- **Support** — Provides healing, buffs, or crowd control

The fourth slot is flexible and should adapt to the content you are tackling. Against bosses, add another DPS. In raids, consider a second support for sustained healing.

### Best Story Progression Team

**Spirit Knight (Tank) + Shadow Blade (DPS) + Celestial Healer (Support) + Flame Archer (Flex DPS)**

This team excels at clearing story chapters efficiently. Spirit Knight draws aggro with his taunt ability, allowing Shadow Blade to deal massive backstab damage. Celestial Healer keeps everyone alive through tough boss encounters, and Flame Archer adds area damage for clearing groups of enemies. The synergy between Spirit Knight's defense debuff and Shadow Blade's critical strike passive creates a devastating damage combo.

Swap Flame Archer for Frost Mage when facing fire-element enemies in later worlds for elemental advantage.

### Best PvP Arena Team

**Shadow Blade (DPS) + Void Assassin (DPS) + Spirit Knight (Tank) + Storm Caller (Support)**

PvP demands burst damage and crowd control. Shadow Blade and Void Assassin form a dual-assassin core that can eliminate key targets before the opponent can react. Spirit Knight provides the needed frontline, while Storm Caller's speed buff lets your assassins act first. Storm Caller's silence ability is critical for shutting down enemy healers.

The key to this team is targeting the enemy healer first, then the enemy DPS. Use Storm Caller's silence on the healer to prevent recovery, then burst them down with both assassins.

### Best Raid Team

**Celestial Healer (Support) + Spirit Knight (Tank) + Flame Archer (DPS) + Frost Mage (Support/DPS)**

Raids are endurance battles that require sustained damage and consistent healing. Celestial Healer is essential for keeping the team alive through the boss's powerful attacks. Spirit Knight's damage reduction aura stacks with Celestial Healer's healing. Flame Archer provides steady DPS from a safe distance, while Frost Mage adds both damage and crowd control with freeze effects.

For the hardest raids, consider replacing Frost Mage with a second Celestial Healer if your DPS stats are high enough from upgrades.

### Best Boss Fight Team

**Shadow Blade (DPS) + Spirit Knight (Tank) + Celestial Healer (Support) + Lightning Samurai (Burst DPS)**

Boss fights are about maximum single-target damage. Shadow Blade's backstab and Lightning Samurai's thunder strike both deal enormous damage to a single target. Spirit Knight tanks the boss while drawing its attacks away from your damage dealers. Celestial Healer sustains the team through the boss's area attacks.

Time Lightning Samurai's ultimate ability for when the boss enters its vulnerable phase — this is when you deal 2x damage and can shorten the fight significantly.

### Team Synergy Tips

- **Element matching** — Always check the enemy's element and bring units with a type advantage. Fire beats Nature, Nature beats Water, Water beats Fire.
- **Ability combos** — Spirit Knight's defense debuff makes Shadow Blade's critical hits even more lethal. Chain your abilities for maximum effect.
- **Speed order** — In PvP, ensure your support acts before your DPS. Use speed buffs or position units accordingly.
- **Upgrade priority** — Always max your main DPS first, then tank, then support. A well-upgraded DPS carries harder than a moderately upgraded full team.

### When to Change Your Team

Switch your team composition when you notice your current setup struggling. Signs you need a change include: dying before the boss reaches 50% HP, running out of healing in raids, or losing more than 50% of PvP matches. Adapt your team to the content, and always check elemental advantages before entering a fight.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best team for beginners in Anime Story 2?**
Start with Spirit Knight, Shadow Blade, Celestial Healer, and any free DPS unit you get from story rewards. This balanced setup will carry you through the first three worlds.

**Can I use the same team for PvP and story?**
While possible, it is not optimal. PvP favors burst and speed, while story favors sustained damage and survival. Build separate teams once you have enough units.

**How many units do I need for a good team?**
You only need 4 active units per team, but having 8-10 well-upgraded units lets you adapt to different content types.

**Does team order matter in Anime Story 2?**
Yes. The first unit in your lineup determines turn order in PvP and who the enemy targets first. Place your tank first and DPS in later positions.
