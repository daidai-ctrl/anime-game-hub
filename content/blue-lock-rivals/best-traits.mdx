## How to Get Good Traits

1. **Trait Reroll** — Use Balls or special items to reroll traits
2. **Farming** — Some game modes drop trait enhancement items
3. **Events** — Limited-time events often reward premium trait items
4. **Evolution Bonuses** — Some evolutions unlock exclusive traits

## Trait Priority Guide

- **DPS Players** — Prioritize damage-boosting traits like Flow State or Monster
- **Support Players** — Look for healing or buff-enhancing traits
- **Hybrid Players** — Balanced traits that improve both offense and defense
