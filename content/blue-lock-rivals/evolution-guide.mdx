## Blue Lock Rivals Evolution Guide (2026) — All Evolutions & Materials

Evolution is the most powerful upgrade system in Blue Lock Rivals. Evolved players gain massive stat boosts and new abilities, making them significantly stronger than their base forms.

## All Evolutions in Blue Lock Rivals

| Base | Evolved | Materials | Bonus |
|------|---------|-----------|-------|
| Isagi | **Isagi Metavision** | 10 Vision Shards + 5 Ego Crystals | 2x Passing, Metavision Skill |
| Nagi | **Nagi Perfect Trap** | 15 Trap Essences + 8 Skill Fragments | 2.5x Ball Control, Perfect Trap |
| Bachira | **Bachira Monster** | 12 Monster Energy + 6 Dribble Shards | 2x Dribbling, Monster Skill |
| Chigiri | **Chigiri Top Speed** | 8 Speed Crystals + 4 Sprint Shards | 2x Speed, Top Speed Skill |
| Kunigami | **Kunigami Power Shot** | 6 Power Stones + 3 Shot Cores | 2x Shooting, Power Shot Skill |

## Evolution Priority

1. **Isagi → Isagi Metavision** — Highest damage increase, essential for endgame
2. **Nagi → Nagi Perfect Trap** — Massive AoE upgrade, great for farming
3. **Bachira → Bachira Monster** — Strong PvP evolution with new skills

## How to Farm Evolution Materials

- **Daily Dungeons** — Consistent source of evolution materials
- **Raid Bosses** — Higher drop rates for rare materials
- **Events** — Limited-time events often provide evolution items
- **Shop** — Some materials can be purchased with Coins

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which player should I evolve first?**
Evolve Isagi first — the Isagi Metavision form is a massive upgrade that carries in all content.

**Can I undo an evolution?**
No, evolutions are permanent. Make sure you want to evolve before committing the materials.
