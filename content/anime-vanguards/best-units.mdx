<h2>Anime Vanguards Best Units (2026)</h2>
<p>Choosing the best units in Anime Vanguards depends on your current progression, available traits, and whether you need damage, support, or farming efficiency. This page summarizes the top units across different roles so you can decide who to build next.</p>

<h2>Best DPS Units</h2>
<p>DPS units are the main source of damage in any team. The best DPS units should deal consistent damage, scale well with upgrades, and perform reliably in difficult stages and boss fights.</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Alucard:</strong> Top-tier damage dealer with strong single-target burst and AoE capability.</li>
  <li><strong>Sung Jinwoo:</strong> Excellent scaling DPS that improves significantly with upgrades and traits.</li>
  <li><strong>Madara:</strong> High burst damage with powerful area attacks for wave clearing.</li>
  <li><strong>Ichigo:</strong> Consistent melee DPS with good survivability and team synergy.</li>
</ul>

<h2>FAQ</h2>

<h3>Who is the best unit in Anime Vanguards?</h3>
<p>Alucard and Sung Jinwoo are generally considered the strongest overall, but the best unit depends on your team composition, traits, and the content you are playing.</p>

<h3>Should I build DPS or support first?</h3>
<p>Most players should build one strong DPS unit first, then add support to keep the team alive in harder stages.</p>

<h3>Are limited units always better than standard units?</h3>
<p>Not always. Some standard units are very efficient for farming and story progression. Limited units may have higher ceilings but require more investment.</p>
