<h2>Anime Vanguards Familiar Guide (2026)</h2>

<p>Familiars are companion units in Anime Vanguards that provide passive bonuses or active abilities to support your main team. Understanding which Familiars to use and how to unlock them can give you an advantage in both PvE and PvP content.</p>

<h2>What Are Familiars?</h2>

<p>Familiars are secondary companions that fight alongside your main units. They may provide stat boosts, additional damage, healing, or utility effects. Some Familiars are permanent, while others are available only during special events or after completing specific challenges.</p>

<h2>How to Unlock Familiars</h2>

<p>Familiars can be unlocked through several methods in Anime Vanguards:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Progress through specific Story Mode chapters.</li>
  <li>Complete Boss Rush milestones for rare Familiar drops.</li>
  <li>Participate in limited-time events for exclusive Familiars.</li>
  <li>Check the <a href="/anime-vanguards/codes">codes page</a> for any Familiar-related rewards.</li>
  <li>Reach specific player level milestones.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Best Familiars for DPS</h2>

<p>DPS-focused Familiars enhance your main damage dealer with additional attacks, critical boosts, or damage amplification:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Attack Familiars:</strong> Add extra damage to your primary carry.</li>
  <li><strong>Critical Familiars:</strong> Increase critical hit rate or critical damage.</li>
  <li><strong>Speed Familiars:</strong> Improve attack speed for faster clears.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Best Familiars for Support</h2>

<p>Support Familiars focus on keeping your team alive and enhancing overall team performance:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Healing Familiars:</strong> Provide passive or active healing during combat.</li>
  <li><strong>Shield Familiars:</strong> Add protective barriers to absorb damage.</li>
  <li><strong>Buff Familiars:</strong> Extend or strengthen team-wide buffs.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Best Familiars for Beginners</h2>

<p>Beginners should focus on Familiars that are easy to unlock and provide general-purpose bonuses. Do not invest heavily in niche Familiars until your core team is stable.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Start with Familiars from early Story Mode chapters.</li>
  <li>Prioritize healing or shield Familiars for survival.</li>
  <li>Upgrade basic Familiars before chasing rare ones.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Familiar Upgrade Priority</h2>

<p>When upgrading Familiars, focus on your most-used companions first. A well-upgraded common Familiar is often more useful than an underleveled rare one.</p>

<ol>
  <li>Upgrade your main DPS Familiar first.</li>
  <li>Then invest in your primary support Familiar.</li>
  <li>Finally, upgrade niche Familiars for specific game modes.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What is the best Familiar in Anime Vanguards?</h3>
<p>The best Familiar depends on your team composition and the game mode you are focusing on. DPS Familiars are popular for Boss Rush, while support Familiars shine in Infinite Mode and difficult story stages.</p>

<h3>Can I use multiple Familiars at once?</h3>
<p>Familiar slots may be limited. Check your current available slots and choose the Familiars that best complement your team setup.</p>

<h3>Are event Familiars worth collecting?</h3>
<p>Yes. Event-exclusive Familiars may offer unique bonuses that are not available through regular gameplay. Collect them while the event is active.</p>

<h3>Should I upgrade Familiars or units first?</h3>
<p>Units should always be prioritized over Familiars. Invest in Familiars only after your main team is well-built and you have spare resources.</p>
