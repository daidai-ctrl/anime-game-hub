## Anime Vanguards Beginner Guide

Starting out in Anime Vanguards? This guide will help you build a strong team, understand the game's systems, and progress efficiently.

## Getting Started

When you first join Anime Vanguards:

1. **Complete the Tutorial** - Learn the basic tower defense mechanics
2. **Redeem All Codes** - Use our codes page to get free gems and resources
3. **Do Your First Summons** - Spend initial gems on the current banner
4. **Play Story Mode** - Complete chapters to earn more gems and experience

## Understanding the Gacha System

### Banner Types
- **Standard Banner** - Always available, contains all base units
- **Rate-Up Banner** - Featured units have increased pull rates
- **Limited Banner** - Time-limited exclusive units

### Summoning Tips
- **Save for Rate-Up Banners** - Better chance at S-tier units
- **Don't spend gems on standard banner** unless you're just starting
- **Pity System** - After a certain number of pulls, you're guaranteed a high-rarity unit
- **Multi-pulls** - Always do 10x pulls for bonus rewards

## Team Building Guide

### Early Game Team
- **1 Main DPS** - Your strongest damage dealer
- **1 Crowd Control** - Unit with AoE or slow effects
- **1 Support** - Buffer or healer
- **Fill the rest** with your next strongest units

### Mid Game Team
- Start focusing on unit evolution
- Build synergy between units
- Prioritize units that work well in Infinite Mode

### Late Game Team
- Full team of evolved S/God-tier units
- Optimized for Boss Rush and Infinite Mode
- Multiple team compositions for different content

## Best Units to Invest In

### Priority Evolution Targets
1. **Gojo** - Becomes God-tier when evolved
2. **Goku MUI** - Highest DPS potential
3. **Naruto Baryon** - Amazing sustain
4. **Luffy Gear 5** - Best crowd control

### Units to Avoid Investing In
- C-tier and D-tier units not worth the resources
- Units without evolution paths
- Dupes of units you don't plan to use

## Farming Guide

### Best Gem Farming Methods
1. **Daily Quests** - Consistent gem income
2. **Story Mode** - One-time gem rewards per chapter
3. **Infinite Mode** - Endless gem potential
4. **Boss Rush** - Gems plus evolution materials
5. **Codes** - Check regularly for new codes

### Resource Management
- **Gems** - Save for rate-up banners
- **Reroll Tokens** - Use on your best units
- **Evolution Materials** - Don't waste on low-tier units
- **Coins** - Used for unit upgrades, fairly common

## Tips and Tricks

- Log in daily for login rewards
- Join a guild for bonus rewards
- Watch for limited-time events with exclusive units
- Check for new codes every week
- Don't rush evolution - make sure you're investing in the right units
- Use Memoria Shards strategically on your core team

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Should I reroll my account?
If you don't get at least one A-tier or higher unit from your initial pulls, it's worth rerolling. Gojo and Goku MUI are the best starting units.

### How do I get evolution materials?
Evolution materials drop from Boss Rush stages and special events. Higher difficulty stages have better drop rates.

### What's the fastest way to get gems?
Complete all story chapters first, then focus on daily quests and Infinite Mode. Don't forget to redeem codes regularly!
