## Anime Vanguards Tier List - May 2026

Our comprehensive tier list ranks every unit in Anime Vanguards based on their performance in Infinite Mode, Boss Rush, and Story content. Updated for Update 5.

## God-Tier Units

The absolute best of the best:

- **Evolved Gojo** - Dominates all game modes with unmatched damage and utility
- **Evolved Goku MUI** - Highest DPS in the game, essential for Boss Rush

## S-Tier Units

Top-tier units that excel in most content:

- **Evolved Naruto Baryon** - Incredible sustain and burst damage
- **Evolved Luffy Gear 5** - Outstanding crowd control and AoE
- **Evolved Madara** - Devastating area attacks
- **Evolved Sukuna** - High damage with excellent range

## A-Tier Units

Strong performers that work well in most teams:

- **Gojo** - Strong base form, great until evolved
- **Goku UI** - High DPS even without evolution
- **Vegeta Ultra Ego** - Counter-attack specialist
- **Ichigo Bankai** - Balanced fighter with good damage
- **Zoro King of Hell** - Excellent melee DPS

## B-Tier Units

Solid picks for mid-game progression:

- **Tanjiro** - Good starter unit
- **Sasuke** - Decent burst damage
- **Levi** - Fast attacker
- **Killua** - Good for speed-based strategies

## C-Tier Units

Niche picks that need investment:

- **Deku** - Needs specific team synergy
- **Bakugo** - Limited range
- **Basic Banner Units** - Outclassed by higher tiers

## D-Tier Units

Replace as soon as possible:

- **Starter Units** - Only useful for very early game
- **Unupgraded Common Units** - Not viable for harder content

## How to Build the Best Team

1. **Focus on one God-tier or S-tier carry** - Prioritize evolving your best unit
2. **Build around your carry** - Add support units that enhance your DPS
3. **Include crowd control** - Units like Luffy Gear 5 help manage waves
4. **Upgrade strategically** - Resources are limited, focus on your core team

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I evolve units?
Evolution requires duplicate copies of the unit plus special evolution materials from Boss Rush and events. Plan your summons accordingly.

### What's the best unit for Infinite Mode?
Evolved Gojo and Evolved Goku MUI are the top picks for Infinite Mode due to their massive damage output and survivability.

### How often is the tier list updated?
We update after every major game update (approximately every 3-4 weeks). Update 5 brought significant balance changes.
