## Daily Routine

For maximum efficiency, follow this daily routine:

1. **Reset**: Complete daily missions first (200+ gems)
2. **Stamina Dump**: Run Story 8-5 for gems until stamina is low
3. **Raids**: Use remaining stamina on raids for materials
4. **Arena**: Do 10 arena battles for weekly points
5. **Events**: Check limited-time events for bonus rewards
