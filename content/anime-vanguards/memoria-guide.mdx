<h2>Anime Vanguards Memoria Guide (2026)</h2>

<p>Memoria is a special system in Anime Vanguards that provides additional bonuses to your units beyond traits and relics. Understanding how Memoria works, which ones to prioritize, and how to unlock them can significantly improve your team performance in Story Mode, Infinite Mode, and Boss Rush.</p>

<h2>What Is Memoria?</h2>

<p>Memoria are special items or equipable bonuses that enhance specific unit stats or abilities. They add another layer of customization on top of traits and relics, allowing players to fine-tune units for specific roles or game modes.</p>

<h2>How to Unlock Memoria</h2>

<p>Memoria can be unlocked through progression milestones, special events, or specific game modes. Some Memoria may require completing certain story chapters, while others are available through limited-time events or boss drops.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Progress through Story Mode to unlock basic Memoria slots.</li>
  <li>Complete Boss Rush challenges for advanced Memoria drops.</li>
  <li>Participate in limited-time events for exclusive Memoria.</li>
  <li>Check the <a href="/anime-vanguards/codes">codes page</a> for any Memoria-related rewards.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Best Memoria for DPS Units</h2>

<p>DPS units benefit most from Memoria that increase damage output, attack speed, or critical hit rate. Prioritize Memoria that directly boost your main damage dealer.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Attack Boost Memoria:</strong> Increases base attack damage.</li>
  <li><strong>Critical Rate Memoria:</strong> Raises the chance of critical hits.</li>
  <li><strong>Skill Damage Memoria:</strong> Enhances ability-specific damage.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Best Memoria for Support Units</h2>

<p>Support units should focus on Memoria that enhance healing, shielding, or buff duration. These Memoria help your team survive longer in difficult content.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Healing Boost Memoria:</strong> Increases healing effectiveness.</li>
  <li><strong>Shield Duration Memoria:</strong> Extends shield and protection effects.</li>
  <li><strong>Buff Extension Memoria:</strong> Makes team buffs last longer.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Best Memoria for Beginners</h2>

<p>Beginners should prioritize Memoria that provide overall stat boosts rather than niche effects. General-purpose Memoria are more useful early in the game when you are still building your core team.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Focus on Memoria with broad stat increases.</li>
  <li>Avoid rare or specialized Memoria until your team is stable.</li>
  <li>Upgrade basic Memoria first, then replace as better options become available.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Upgrade Memoria</h2>

<p>Memoria can be upgraded using specific materials found in farming stages, events, or boss rewards. Higher-level Memoria provide stronger bonuses but require more resources.</p>

<h3>What is the best Memoria in Anime Vanguards?</h3>
<p>The best Memoria depends on the unit and game mode. DPS units benefit from attack and critical Memoria, while supports prefer healing and buff extension Memoria.</p>

<h3>Can I equip multiple Memoria on one unit?</h3>
<p>Memoria slots may unlock as you progress. Check your unit details to see how many Memoria slots are currently available.</p>

<h3>Should I upgrade Memoria or traits first?</h3>
<p>Both are important, but traits often provide more consistent value. Upgrade traits first if resources are limited, then invest in Memoria for additional optimization.</p>

<h3>Are limited-event Memoria worth collecting?</h3>
